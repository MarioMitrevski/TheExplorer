Hi, I’m Mario, I’m a web developer, dreamer, nomad and travel addict from Skopje in Macedonia.


I quit my job in Dec 2012 and have been traveling slowly, independently and on a budget around Central and South America, West Europe and Australia ever since. I fell in love with Brazil and am now based there in Rio de Janeiro.


I set up this blog to share my tips and experiences for traveling and working abroad to inspire and help you to travel more too.
